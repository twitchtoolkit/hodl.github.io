TwitchToolkit

TwitchToolkit is a mod written by Twitch.tv/hodlhodl that integrates storytelling decisions into chat votes, awards viewers coins for watching, and those coins can be spent on items/events in game.

  • !bal - check balance and karma rate
  • !buy - Syntax: !buy beer 6 - purchase an item or event
  • !joinqueue - join the viewer name queue
  • !whatiskarma - explains what the karma system is
  • !purchaselist - gives users a link to the public purchase list
  • !instructions - gives users info on using the mod
  • !modinfo - gives users info about the mod
  • !modsettings - gives user info on how many coins are being earned and global settings
  • !giftcoins - Syntax: !giftcoins @username 100 - give another viewer your coins
  • !leave - get the HELL OUT (No refunds)
  • !reviveme - revive your pawn
  • !healme - heal your pawn
  • !levelskill [skill] [coins] - level a skill of yours with coins (1500 minimum)
  • !passionshuffle [skill] - Shuffles your passions. Selected skill has a guaranteed minor passion
  • !trait [trait] - add specified trait to your pawn
  • !removetrait [trait] - remove specified trait from your pawn
  • !backpack [item] - this will attempt to purchase items directly into your pawn's inventory
  • !surgery [item] [quantity/bodypart] - Purchases the part specified and queues it for surgery on your pawn.
  • !mypawnstory - view information about your pawns backstory
  • !mypawnskills - view information about your pawns skills
  • !mypawnstats [category] - categorized information. use Basics, Combat, Misc, or Social
  • !mypawnneeds - information about a pawn’s current needs
  • !mypawnrelations - view information about your relationship to other viewers
  • !mypawnbody - displays information about a pawn’s body
  • !mypawngear - Lists all weapons and apparel
  • !mypawnhealth - Lists a health summary
  • !mypawnhealth [system] - Detailed information about a pawns health. Ex. !mypawnhealth sight
  • !mypawnbody - Lists all damage or replaced body parts
  • !colonists - the current number of colonists
  • !wealth - colony's current wealth
  • !insult [target] - insult another colonist (sometimes)
  • !research - shows the current research in progress
  • !factions - lists the colony's relationship with other factions
  • !reviveall - colony wide ressurection
  • !healall- colony wide healing
